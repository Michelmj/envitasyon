Our Story
00 Where It All Began

Some love stories begin with fireworks. Ours began with two children growing up around the same families, never realizing that God was quietly writing a story that would unfold years later. For years, the Michel and Jean Baptiste families celebrated milestones, worshipped together, and supported one another. Although their families were close, Mario and Sephora were never childhood sweethearts. Mario was the cheerful little boy who laughed at everything, while Sephora was the shy little girl who preferred to stay in the background.

01 A Friendship Reunited

In 2020, they reconnected at a church dedication in Palm Beach. Sephora almost missed the event because she wasn't feeling well, but when she arrived, conversation came effortlessly. They spent the afternoon laughing together, and when someone jokingly asked if they were dating, they both laughed and answered, "No!" Looking back, that moment feels almost prophetic. Their friendship continued through countless phone calls about family, faith, work, and life. In 2021, they reunited at Sephora's sister's wedding, where Mario drove them together. Afterward, their conversations grew longer, and their friendship deepened.

02 From Friendship to Love

In 2022, while Sephora was volunteering with a Catholic charitable organization in Arizona, Mario surprised her by flying there after a trip to the Dominican Republic. Pretending he needed her address to mail her something, he instead appeared at her front door. Together they explored Arizona, and a month later, Sephora visited Dallas, where Mario happily became her personal tour guide (Museums, the zoo, and road trips). They created an unforgettable adventure through Oklahoma's Wichita Mountains, where the summer heat nearly convinced them they wouldn't survive. Years later, they still debate whose idea that hike really was. By 2023, Mario realized his best friend had become the woman he wanted to marry. Sephora agreed to give their relationship a chance, and throughout 2024 they intentionally built their future together despite the distance.

03 The Beginning of Forever

In the summer of 2025, surrounded by the same families who had witnessed the beginning of their story years earlier, Mario asked Sephora to spend forever with him. With overwhelming joy, she said yes. Looking back, they know their love was built through friendship, laughter, faith, sacrifice, and God's perfect timing. They are grateful for every step that brought them here and are excited to begin their next chapter together—surrounded by the family and friends who have been part of their journey from the very beginning.
